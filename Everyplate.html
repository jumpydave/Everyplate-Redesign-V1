About Us

EveryPlate is a flexible meal kit subscription service.

Each week, we deliver fresh, quality ingredients and simple recipes to your door, making it easy to cook fuss-free, filling and tasty dinners. Each recipe has easy-to-follow instructions that put you in control of dinner!

We're super flexible, so you can skip a week or cancel your subscription any time before your weekly order cut-off. You can find your weekly order cut-off in the  ‘My Menu’ section of your account.
